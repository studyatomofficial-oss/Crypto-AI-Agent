For an AI demo, I recommend **Hinglish**. It sounds much more natural to Indian business owners than pure Hindi or pure English.

Here's a polished training script:

💡 **Suggestion:** Instead of immediately selling, let the AI amaze the listener first. For example, after introducing itself, Priya could ask:

> "Before I tell you about our services, can I ask you a question? Agar maine pehle na bataya hota ki main AI hoon, to kya aapko lagta ki aap ek real person se baat kar rahe hain?"

This creates curiosity, demonstrates the AI's realism, and makes the sales pitch much more memorable.
